Vice President of Activities

Cornell Best Buddies

The Cornell Best Buddies chapter works to promote inclusion and friendships between Cornell students and people with intellectual and developmental disabilities in the Ithaca area. As VP of Activities, my role consists of planning all group events, both in-person and virtual, during the semester and working with the rest of the board to fundraise, grow our chapter, and continue to promote our message on campus.

Through Cornell's Raising Education Attainment Challenge (REACH) program, I have worked as a tutor at Ithaca elementary and middle schools. My role involves helping students understand and improve upon various subjects, such as math and reading.

Juni Learning is an organization that connects young students interested in computer science with more experienced programmers. As an instructor, I work with several students between the ages of ten and fifteen on a weekly basis, teaching them basic skills in Python, Java, and Scratch.
