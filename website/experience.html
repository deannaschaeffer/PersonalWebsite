My Positions

Software Engineering Intern

As a software engineering intern on the Product-Led Growth team, I collaborated with three other engineers to develop a feature that surfaces users' unmonitored data sources. I utilized React, TypeScript, and JavaScript to contribute various frontend components in order to help with accessing and displaying this feature. Vice President of Activities

The Cornell Best Buddies chapter works to promote inclusion and friendships between Cornell students and people with intellectual and developmental disabilities in the Ithaca area. As VP of Activities, my role consists of planning all group events, both in-person and virtual, during the semester and working with the rest of the board to fundraise, grow our chapter, and continue to promote our message on campus. Some of the events I have planned include painting events, mini golf, scavenger hunts, and movie nights.
